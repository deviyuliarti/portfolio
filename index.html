PalpAid

Multimodal Pneumatic Tactile Sensor for Tissue Palpation

Robot-assisted surgery often reduces touch to vision alone. PalpAid fills this sensory desert with a simple, low-profile, low-cost pneumatic tactile sensor that leverages contact dynamics: contact force induces an internal pressure change (pressure = event detection) and a distinctive acoustic signature (microphone) to support tissue delineation.
